Her story:

I was looking for "forever" - I was always looking for "forever" - and when I met Tim, I found it.

On Aug. 31, 2009, I was matched with him and about 10 other men on eHarmony. After briefly looking through profiles, I deleted all but Tim's and figured, "If he contacts me, then we'll see where it goes."

I got a message the next day.

Those first couple of weeks, we messaged each other on eHarmony, and then ... I gave him my phone number. When he called I felt like I was 15 again - "Don't giggle too much, Ali," "Did you really just say that, Ali?" "Don't forget to breathe."

And then we decided to meet.

Now, I know that life is not a movie. There is no background music, and I wasn't expecting Tim to arrive on a white horse or anything. But when I saw him for the first time, I swear it happened in slow motion. The world stopped for those few seconds, and I really did fall in love.

His story:

I was at a point in my life where I wanted to find someone to share my life with. I'd heard about online dating, but I had never thought about trying it until I learned that a family member of mine was successful using eHarmony.

After some research ... I signed up.

I received several matches, but none of them really seemed to be what I was looking for. Then I was matched with Ali. I worked up the courage to send her a message hoping she would reply. To my excitement, she did.

As we communicated, I could tell that this was something special. She seemed to be my perfect match. When we finally met, I knew instantly that I had found "the one."
