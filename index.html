Who am I?

My name is Allison Logan. I am a copy editor, designer and front-end web developer currently working for The New York Times Co. In 2009, I graduated from the University of Florida with a bachelor's degree in journalism with a focus on editing and web development. I will graduate from the University of Florida in December 2015 with my master's degree in journalism with a specialization in web design and online communication.

I specialize in copy editing news and feature stories; designing newspapers, graphics and illustrations; and designing and developing interactive, responsive websites. My web development focus is mainly on the front end with HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery and limited PHP.

I have been working in journalism and web development since early high school. Some of my skills include excellent use of all Adobe programs, Microsoft programs, Git, SEO, analytics, coding, training and beta testing.

I am always open to exciting opportunities, with my resume available for download.
