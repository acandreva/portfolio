Who am I?

My name is Allison Logan. I am a marketing and communications specialist working for the University of Florida and a freelance front-end web developer. I graduated from UF in 2009 with a bachelor's degree in journalism focusing on editing and web development and in 2015 with my master's degree in mass communication focusing on web design, branding and online communication.

I specialize in copy editing news and feature stories; designing newspapers, graphics and illustrations; designing and developing interactive, responsive websites; and project management including agile methodologies using programs such as JIRA and Trello. My web development focus is mainly on the front end with HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery and limited PHP.

I have been working in journalism and web development since early high school. Some of my skills include excellent use of all Adobe programs, Microsoft programs, Git, SEO, analytics, coding, social media, training, project management and beta testing.

I am always open to exciting opportunities, with my resume available for download.
