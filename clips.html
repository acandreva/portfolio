These are all of the different articles that I have gotten published, or documents that I have edited or designed. I have also covered events and taken pictures, but I have not displayed them here.

The New York Times Editing Center:

As an employee for The New York Times Editing Center, I work as a copy editor or deputy editor for the News Service. I have also worked on a weekly product called the Special Reports and on the International Daily and the Times Digest.

The Lake City Reporter:

The Lake City Reporter is a six-day newspaper that publishes a variety of news. While there, I was the copy editor and A-section designer. Occasionally, I took photos for the paper and edited Currents Magazine.

Tower Publications Inc.:

Tower Publications Inc. publishes of a variety of weekly, monthly and quarterly magazines. My articles and photographs were published in Senior Times Magazine and Our Town Magazine. I also spent time in the office helping with copy editing and other tasks.

The Satellite:

I interned at The Satellite, an arts and culture magazine in Gainesville, Fla., in 2008. I worked on the magazine's website, contacted photographers, copy edited, complied the monthly calendar, took pictures and wrote articles.
