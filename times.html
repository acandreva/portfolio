The New York Times Editing Center:

Each day, the News Service edits about 100-150 stories from The Times and sends about 40-50, 400-word expresses. Occasionally, I help with compiling the sports budget or business cover.

I have also worked on a weekly product called the Special Reports, where I selected 24-30 feature stories that have already been sent out on the wire. I then edited them for style and made metric conversions, before sending them out to international clients.

Other products I have worked on are the now defunct International Daily and the Times Digest.
